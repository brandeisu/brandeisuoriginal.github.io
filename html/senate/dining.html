Dining Committee

The Senate Dining Committee is the means through which student voices can be appropriately heard by both the Brandeis administration, and by Sodexo. The concerns, suggestions, and ideas of actual students are at the forefront of our work, and we always have student's interests at heart when performing our duties. This past semester has proven to be one of the most transparent, productive, and positive semesters for the committee. We have increased dialogue with the administration, have hosted public events for students to directly raise their concerns, and have made a lot of progress with dining services. We're looking forward to finishing this year off with an amazing semester!
~Ben Margolin, Chair

Getting Involved

We encourage students to continue posting on our facebook group, and reaching out to us when the have a comment or concern. Goals

  1. Influencing the changing meal plans
  2. Provide more dialogue between students, the committee, and Brandeis administration
  3. Help ensure that this summer's Sherman renovations run smoothly
  4. Improve transparency

Last Semester's Accomplishments

  • We held a dining forum to directly connect students with our administration.
  • We changed the amount of meal periods from 3 to 5, thereby allowing for students to swipe more often and use more meals.
  • C-store labeling has improved; including with prices and allergen information.
  • We have improved food variety and diversity.
  • There is now dinner on Saturday nights for kosher students.
  • We hosted focus groups to discuss the future of our meal plan system.
  • We have cooperated with campus clubs, especially Food Recovery Network to improve our dining experience.
  • We have begun an ingredient bible.
  • The dishwasher has been fixed on the non-kosher side of Sherman.
  • Composting and recycling options are now available in Sherman.
